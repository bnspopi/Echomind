"# EchoMind Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 12, 2026

Welcome to EchoMind (\"we\", \"our\", \"us\"). Your privacy and emotional safety are the foundation of this app. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we protect it, and the choices you have.

By using EchoMind, you agree to the practices described below.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### a) Information you provide
- **Account information:** Your name, email address, and a securely hashed password when you create an account.
- **Journal entries:** The reflections, thoughts, and notes you write inside the Journal feature.
- **Mood check-ins:** The mood, optional notes, and timestamps you log.
- **AI chat conversations:** The messages you send to the EchoMind AI companion.
- **Daily intentions:** Short intention statements you set for the day.

### b) Information collected automatically
- Approximate timestamps of when you use the app (to display history and reset counters).
- Basic device crash data if the app unexpectedly fails (no personally identifying info).

### c) Information we do **NOT** collect
- We do **not** collect your phone number, contacts, photos, precise location, advertising ID, or any biometric data.
- We do **not** use third-party analytics or advertising trackers.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information **only** to:
- Provide and improve the core EchoMind features (journal, AI chat, mood tracker, intentions).
- Generate empathetic AI reflections on your journal entries (via secure AI providers — see Section 4).
- Generate optional calming visuals for your mood check-ins.
- Keep your account secure and recover access if you forget your password.

We will **never** sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with brokers.

---

## 3. How Your Data is Stored

- All data is stored in encrypted databases on secure cloud infrastructure.
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt — we cannot see or recover your plaintext password.
- Communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.

---

## 4. Third-Party AI Providers

To power the AI companion and mood visuals, EchoMind sends the relevant message text or mood label to trusted AI providers:
- **Anthropic (Claude)** — for journal reflections and AI chat responses.
- **Google (Gemini)** — for AI-generated mood visuals.

These providers process the data **transiently** to generate a response and do **not** train their models on your data when accessed through our integration. We send only the content needed for that single request — never your name, email, or other identifying information.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

EchoMind is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

---

## 6. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:
- **Access** the data we hold about you.
- **Edit or delete** any journal entry, mood check-in, or chat message from within the app.
- **Delete your entire account** along with all associated data — email us and we will remove your data within 30 days.
- **Export** your data in a portable format on request.

---

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we permanently delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is legally required.

---

## 8. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encryption-in-transit, secure password hashing, JWT-based authentication, and least-privilege access controls. No system is 100% secure, but we work hard to protect you.

---

## 9. Important Note — Not Medical Advice

EchoMind is a wellness companion, **not** a medical or mental health treatment service. The AI is not a therapist, doctor, or crisis service. If you are experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact a qualified professional or emergency services in your area immediately (e.g., **988** in the US, **112** in the EU, **iCall 9152987821** in India).

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. If we make material changes, we'll notify you in-app or by email before the changes take effect. The \"Last updated\" date at the top will always reflect the latest version.

---
